WikiMaps
  • An open source Chrome extension for aggregation, display, and information regarding user-selected locations on any webpage, powered by Google Maps and Wikipedia.
  • During my semester abroad, I relied on maps extensively to find my way around Europe. As I was planning trips I would often find myself wanting to quickly see a number of locations together on one map to see how close together they were. After stumbling across this article about monuments scattered around Eastern Europe, I decided it was time to use programming to solve my problem.
  • WikiMaps allows users to select pieces of text on a webpage and add them to a list of locations stored locally in their browser. The extension then attempts to geolocate the text and pull relevant Wikipedia information. The information is then displayed on a Google Map where the user can add, remove, and view their locations.
